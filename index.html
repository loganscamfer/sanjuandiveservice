Our Services

Underwater Services

Serving boat owners, marinas, waterfront properties, and operators in the marine industry.

Hull Cleaning & Boat Bottom Maintenance

Full hull cleaning, marine growth removal, zinc replacement, intake screen cleaning, and condition reporting for vessels of all sizes.

Prop Installation & Servicing

Underwater propeller installation, replacement, and servicing for outboard and sterndrive systems.

Mooring Buoy Installation, Inspection & Repair

Installation of new mooring systems, chain & shackle replacement, anchor block checks, and annual safety inspections.

Aquatic Weed & Vegetation Control

Diver-assisted removal of invasive aquatic vegetation around docks, marinas, and waterfront properties. Helps maintain safe and clear waterways.

Dock & Floating Dock Inspections

Structural inspections for floats, pilings, anchorage, underwater connections, and hardware. Professional documentation available upon request.

Personal Item Recovery & More

Salvage assistance, object recovery, line detangling, and more. Contact us for specific project inquiries.
